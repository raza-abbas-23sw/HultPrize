What is Hult Prize?

The Hult Prize is a global movement empowering young people to create social enterprises that address the world’s most pressing challenges. Known as the "Nobel Prize for Students," the Hult Prize inspires thousands of students worldwide to develop innovative solutions for a better tomorrow. Its mission is to build a generation of leaders who transform the world into a sustainable and equitable place.
